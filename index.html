How do you celebrate your software on Devpost? Create a portfolio and tell the story behind your code. Explain what inspired you, how you and your team worked together, the obstacles you ran into, and how you overcame them.

Landing pages sell and readmes are instructional, but it's the stories that people care about.

There are hundreds of thousands of developers out there who are eager to learn from you and your experiences. And they're on Devpost too.

Our motto is “Devpost or it didn't happen.” Because, if you don't tell anyone about your software, how will people ever find out about it? Nobody can explain your hacks better than you!

We're not PR for pre-launch startups, a crowd-funding site, SaaS, link aggregator, or hype machine. We are a supportive community of hackers who love building and learning (and emoji). Even if your project isn't finished, share your progress and keep your followers up to date.
